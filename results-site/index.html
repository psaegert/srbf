About these numbers

These series are re-rendered through srbf.analysis from the archived Flash-ANSR v23.0 evaluation (the first paper's results), not yet a fresh canonical run. Flash-ANSR series carry version v23.0; symbolic-data, srbf, and the catalog artifacts are - (they did not exist at eval time). The version travels with each series in the legend.

The compute axis is median per-problem wall-clock fit time, the one budget comparable across methods (Flash-ANSR sweeps choices, PySR iterations, NeSymReS the beam width, E2E the candidate count; time unifies them). Metrics are the strict is_perfect_fit numeric recovery, token-level skeleton F1, and median log10 FVU; symbolic recovery is omitted until predictions are normalized to the current simplify.
